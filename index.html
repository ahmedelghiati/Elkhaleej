ABOUT US

Elkhaleej for import & export is a global import-export company of fresh fruit and vegatables , an Egyptian Company . It also has offices in Senegal , Benin and Qatar. The firm has more than 20 years of experience in the fresh fruit and vegatables business. Elkhaleej for import & export has a wide portfolio of over 25 produce items, but focuses on garlic, lemons, onion, potatoes, carrot and an orange. The company works with more than 30 loyal and 200 customers in 25 countries. Elkhaleej for import & export was founded by Reda Aboaish

What We Do?

Land Management

Our commitment to sustainable land management is the foundation of our success. We meticulously plan and care for the land where our fruits and vegetables are grown, ensuring healthy soils, efficient water usage, and minimal environmental impact. By prioritizing responsible land stewardship, we not only guarantee the highest quality produce but also contribute to a greener, more sustainable planet.

Production

Our dedication to excellence in production is evident in every piece of fruit and vegetable we export. We employ cutting-edge agricultural practices and the latest technology to cultivate crops that are bursting with flavor, nutrition, and visual appeal. From carefully selected seedlings to precise harvesting techniques, our focus on production quality is unwavering.

Packing

At our state-of-the-art packing facilities, we take pride in the art of packaging. Our skilled team ensures that each piece of produce is handled with care and attention to detail. We employ the latest packaging innovations to extend shelf life, maintain freshness, and reduce waste. When you receive our products, you can trust that they are not only delicious but also impeccably packaged for your convenience.

Shipping

From our fields to your table, our shipping process is a well-orchestrated symphony. We understand the importance of timeliness and reliability in global food distribution. Our efficient shipping logistics ensure that your orders arrive on schedule and in the pristine condition you expect. With our extensive network, we can reach every corner of the world, bringing the bounty of our farms to your doorstep.
